Problem Definations

In 2022, Brunei Darussalam is reported with staggering 50,000 stray cats and dogs. The losts of beloved animals, stray cats and dogs need an effective and efficient ways of sharing or receiving information in real-time to public to help or rescue the animals. This application would provide public and animal lovers platform to keep track of stray cats, dogs and lost animals.

Proposed functional Requirements

  • Map to allow user to keep tracks on the location of founded lost animals and nearby veterinians
  • User are able to create, update, delete posts
  • View list of near by veterinians and caregivers
  • Notification to alert user of founded animals
  • Search filters based on locations,breeds,cats,or dogs
  • Login and Registration
